Our Vision

At Claws and Paws, our vision is a world where wildlife thrives in harmony with communities. We aim to protect endangered species, restore habitats, and empower local people to become stewards of nature through sustainable conservation practices.

Our Mission

Our mission is to safeguard wildlife and their habitats by promoting awareness, engaging communities, and driving sustainable conservation efforts. We believe in the power of education, collaboration, and action to create lasting impact for both nature and people.

Our Leadership

Claws and Paws is led by a team of passionate conservationists committed to protecting wildlife and empowering local communities. Their experience, dedication, and love for nature drive the heart of this project forward and give a brighter future to endangered animals.
